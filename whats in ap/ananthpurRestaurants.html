Hotel Rajahasma

Hotel Rajahamsa in Anantapur is a charming 2.5-star hotel located just 0.5 km from the city center. It offers a range of amenities including free WiFi, free parking, and room service. Guests can enjoy an indoor pool, a rooftop pool, and a restaurant on-site.

Key Features:

Location: Close to popular attractions like Ravadurg Gooty Fort and Prasanthi Nilayam.

Amenities:

Free WiFi and parking, Indoor and rooftop pools, Restaurant serving various cuisines, 24-hour front desk and room service, Business center and convention facilities.

Guest Reviews:

Many guests appreciate the hotel's cleanliness and friendly staff. The indoor pool is often highlighted as a great feature for relaxation. Some reviews mention the convenience of its location for exploring nearby attractions.

Blue Moon Highway

Blue Moon Highway Restaurant in Anantapur is a popular dining spot known for its diverse menu, which includes Indian, Chinese, and Italian cuisines. The restaurant is well-rated, with a focus on providing a comfortable dining experience and good service.

The Grand Inn

The Grand Inn in Anantapur is a luxury hotel located near Srikantam Circle, offering a comfortable stay for both families and corporate travelers.

Accommodation: The hotel provides spacious rooms equipped with modern amenities, ensuring a pleasant experience for guests.

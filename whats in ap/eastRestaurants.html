Subbayya Hotel

A well-known spot famous for its authentic Andhra cuisine, particularly its biryanis and thalis.

Subbayya Hotel, also known as Subbayya Gari Hotel, is a renowned chain of vegetarian restaurants based in Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh. Established in 1950, it began as a small mess serving meals for just 0.50 paisa. Over the years, it has expanded significantly and is now famous for its authentic Andhra cuisine, particularly its signature dish, Butta Bhojanam.

Suruchi Grand is a multi-cuisine restaurant and banquet hall located in East Godavari. It has garnered positive reviews, with a recommendation rate of 96% based on customer feedback. Sitara Multicuisine

Sitara Multicuisine in East Godavari is a popular dining destination known for its spacious seating, quick service, and excellent food. Here are some key points about the restaurant:

Cuisine: Sitara offers a diverse menu that includes a variety of Indian and international dishes, catering to different tastes. Signature dishes include Chicken Biryani, Mughlai specialties, and a selection of vegetarian options.

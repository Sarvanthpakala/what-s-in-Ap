Castle “Mandi” Arabic Restaurant

Castle “Mandi” Arabic Restaurant in Guntur is known for its generous portions and a variety of mandi dishes, which are traditional Arabic rice dishes typically served with meat. The restaurant features a comfortable dining environment, making it suitable for casual meals and celebrations. It is located in Brodipet, G untur, and is recognized for its whimsical decor that enhances the dining experience.

Venkatesh Grand

Venkatesh Grand in Guntur is a well-known multi-cuisine restaurant and food delivery service located in Arundalpet. Established in 2007, it has built a reputation for offering a diverse menu that caters to various tastes. The restaurant provides dining options, takeaway, and home delivery services, making it convenient for customers.

Barkaas

Barkaas Restaurant in Guntur is a popular Indo-Arabic dining spot located in Brindavan Gardens, near NTR Stadium. Known for its tasty biryani and a variety of Middle-Eastern dishes, it offers a pocket-friendly menu with a clean environment and good management.

Cuisine: The restaurant offers a variety of traditional Andhra-style dishes, with a strong emphasis on seafood. Popular items include chicken l ollipops, apolo fish, and biryani, all of which have received positive reviews for their taste and quality.

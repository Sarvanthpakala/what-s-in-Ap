Ranganath Swamy Temple

The Ranganath Swamy Temple, also known as Sri Talpagiri Ranganathaswamy Temple, is a prominent Hindu temple located in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh. It is dedicated to Lord Ranganatha, a reclining form of Lord Vishnu. The temple is situated on the banks of the River Pennar and is renowned for its historical and architectural significance.

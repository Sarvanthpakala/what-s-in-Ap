Zeebu Multi-Themed Restaurant

Zeebu Multi-Themed Restaurant in Ongole is a unique dining experience that features various themes, including jungle and flight, and is notable for its robot service. It has received positive reviews for its fresh ambiance and tasty food. The restaurant is designed to be family-friendly, making it particularly enjoyable for children who are fascinated by the robot servers. Here are some key highlights:

Unique Features

Robot Service: The restaurant employs robots to serve food, enhancing the dining experience with a futuristic touch.

Thematic Ambiance: It offers different themes such as jungle and flight, creating an engaging environment for diners.

Kritunga Family Restaurant

Kritunga Family Restaurant, located in Ramnagar, Ongole, is known for its authentic South Indian cuisine and vibrant atmosphere. It offers a diverse menu that showcases the rich flavors of Indian dishes, making it a popular choice for both locals and visitors.

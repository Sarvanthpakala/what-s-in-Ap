Kalingapatnam Beach

Kalingapatnam Beach is a scenic coastal destination located in the district Srikakulam of Andhra Pradesh, India. It's situated on the East coast of the Bay of Bengal, approximately 25 km from Nellore city. The beach is known for its pristine seawater,golden brown sand, and picturesque views.

Features and Activities:

The beach offers a peaceful and serene atmosphere,
perfect for relaxation and sunbathing.
Visitors can enjoy early morning and evening walks on the beach, watching the sunrise or sunset. The beach is also suitable for water sports, such as Jet Skiing, which is promoted by the Andhra Pradesh Tourism Department.

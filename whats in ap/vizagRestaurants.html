Garam Masala

Garam masala is a fragrant spice blend commonly used in Indian cuisine. The term "garam" means "hot" or "warm," while "masala" refers to a mixture of spices. This blend typically includes ingredients like cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, cumin, and coriander, but the specific composition can vary widely based on regional and personal preferences.Garam masala originates from the Indian subcontinent and is prevalent in Indian, Pakistani, Nepalese, Bangladeshi, Sri Lankan, and Caribbean cuisines.

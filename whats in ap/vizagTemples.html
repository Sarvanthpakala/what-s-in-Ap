Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

Deity: Lord Lakshmi Narasimha (an avatar of Lord Vishnu) Significance: This ancient temple is located on the Simhachalam hill and is one of the most important pilgrimage sites in the region. The temple is renowned for its intricate architecture and is especially popular during the festival of Chandanotsavam, when the deity is adorned with sandalwood paste. Location: Approximately 16 km from Visakhapatnam city.

Sri Varaha Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy

Deity: Lord Varaha Narasimha Significance: This temple is dedicated to Lord Varaha (another avatar of Vishnu) and is located near the beach at Bheemunipatnam. It is known for its serene environment and beautiful surroundings. Location: About 24 km from Visakhapatnam.

Kailasagiri Hill Temple

Deity: Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati Significance: Located on Kailasagiri Hill, this temple offers panoramic views of the city and the coastline. It features a large statue of Lord Shiva and is a popular tourist destination. Location: Situated within Visakhapatnam city

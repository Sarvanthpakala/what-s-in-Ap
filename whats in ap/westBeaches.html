Perupalem Beach

Perupalem Beach is a scenic coastal destination located in the district West Godavari of Andhra Pradesh, India. It's situated on the East coast of the Bay of Bengal, approximately 25 km from Nellore city. The beach is known for its pristine seawater,golden brown sand, and picturesque views.

Features and Activities:

The beach offers a peaceful and serene atmosphere,
perfect for relaxation and sunbathing.
Visitors can enjoy early morning and evening walks on the beach, watching the sunrise or sunset. The beach is also suitable for water sports, such as Jet Skiing, which is promoted by the Andhra Pradesh Tourism Department.

Naraspur Beach

Naraspur Beach, located near East Godavari in Andhra Pradesh, is known for its golden sands and tranquil atmosphere. It offers a clean and serene environment, making it a hidden gem for visitors. The beach is open year-round and is approximately 20 kilometers from the city center. It's a great spot for those seeking privacy and a peaceful retreat.

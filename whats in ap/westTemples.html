Sri Sitarama Swamy Temple

Deity: Lord Rama Significance: This temple is one of the most famous in Andhra Pradesh and is dedicated to Lord Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana. It is situated on the banks of the Godavari River and is a major pilgrimage site, especially during the festival of Rama Navami. Location: Approximately 120 km from Eluru, the district headquarters.

Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

Deity: Lord Narasimha (an avatar of Lord Vishnu) Significance: This temple is dedicated to Lord Lakshmi Narasimha and is known for its beautiful architecture and spiritual significance. It attracts many devotees, especially during festivals. Location: Located in Narasapur, West Godavari.

Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple

Deity: Lord Venkateswara Significance: This temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara and is a significant pilgrimage site for devotees. It is known for its vibrant festivals and rituals. Location: Situated in Kovvur, West Godavari.
